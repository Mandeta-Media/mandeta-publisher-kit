## The Dual-Engine Revenue Model

Mandeta optimizes two revenue streams simultaneously:

### Engine 1 — Domestic Market (Volume & Precision)
Local advertisers (banking, telecom, retail) form the foundation 
of the ecosystem. Through audience-centric buying and behavioral 
segmentation, we transform bulk traffic into qualified inventory — 
justifying premium rates for every local impression served.

### Engine 2 — International Yield Export (Premium Liquidity)
A significant portion of Albanian publisher traffic originates 
from the DACH region (Germany, Austria, Switzerland) and beyond. 
This diaspora inventory is currently undervalued when served 
through standard international SSPs.

Through our strategic partnerships with leading global media 
conglomerates, Mandeta classifies diaspora traffic as premium 
international inventory — aligning CPM rates with Western European 
standards rather than local averages.

The result: a resilient, diversified revenue stream that balances 
local consistency with international margin.

## The Technology: Proprietary Header Bidding Architecture

At the core of the Mandeta network is a unified auction layer — 
a significant upgrade over legacy waterfall systems.

### How Legacy Systems Fail You
Traditional waterfall models offer your inventory to demand 
partners one at a time, sequentially. This creates revenue 
leakage — the highest bidder doesn't always win, and slow 
partners block faster ones.

### How Mandeta Works
Mandeta's header bidding wrapper runs a **unified auction**, 
allowing multiple demand sources — local DSPs and our 
international exchange partners — to bid simultaneously on a 
single impression. True price discovery. Fair market value for 
every impression served.

### The Bidder-Adapter Framework
Every international demand partner operates on different technical 
protocols. Our proprietary Bidder-Adapter acts as a universal 
translator — instantly converting your inventory data into the 
precise format required by buyers in Zurich, Berlin, or New York.

No complex integration work on your end. Every qualified advertiser 
participates in your auction immediately.

### Performance Standards
- **Asynchronous loading** — bids execute in the background without 
  blocking page rendering, preserving Core Web Vitals and UX
- **Intelligent timeouts** — slow demand partners are bypassed 
  automatically, keeping your site performance optimal
- **Real-time reporting** — live dashboard with fill rates, eCPM, 
  and revenue visibility

## Integration Roadmap

Joining the network is a straightforward, fully managed process:

1. **Contract** — signing of the Master Services Agreement (MSA)
2. **Authorization** — update of `ads.txt` to authorize Mandeta 
   as a certified seller
3. **Deployment** — implementation of the Header Bidding Wrapper

5. **Go live** — 100% traffic allocation and revenue optimization

Our technical team handles everything. You approve, we build.
